OurMission

A professional non-equity theater organization passionately committed to inspire, entertain, and develop productions that promote the diversity of our community and to provide theater opportunities for artists of all ages.

OurVision

To recognize and respect local audiences and artists by offering a collaborate venue for individual growth.

OurValues

  • Community partnerships that reflect the diversity we serve
  • Integrity and professionalism in delivering a quality experience
  • Innovation and experimentation in presenting a wide range of subjects and performance styles
